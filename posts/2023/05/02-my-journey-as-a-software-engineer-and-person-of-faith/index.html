My Journey as a Software Engineer and Person of Faith

2 May 2023·260 words·2 mins
personal life adventure

Hello and welcome to my very first blog post! I’m new to blogging, and I want to share what this blog will be about. My plans might change as I learn more, but I’m excited to start this journey with you.

I have a few main goals for this blog, which I am aware will likely change over-time:

  1. Sharing My Thoughts and Experiences: This blog is where I will talk about my daily life and what I learn. I want to remember these moments and share them with you.

  2. Talking About My Work as a Software Engineer: I’m a Software Engineer, and I want to show what I can do. I’ll also share my thoughts on the latest news in my field.

  3. My Life as a Parent and Spouse: I love my family and want to share our stories. I’ll write about our happy times, challenges, and what we learn together.

  4. Keeping Up with Technology: Technology is always changing, and I want to learn and grow with it. I hope that writing will help me stay up-to-date and useful in the future.

  5. My Faith: I believe in God, and I try to live a good life. I’m not perfect, but I want to share my journey with you and keep trying to be a better person.

I’m excited to start my blog and see where it takes me. I hope you’ll join me on this adventure, and we can learn and grow together. Let’s enjoy this journey, face challenges, and discover new things about ourselves and the world around us!
